About me

I'm a Data Analyst with experience in developing end-to-end projects, from data generation to reporting, using various tools including Python, SQL, and Tableau.

I completed my graduate studies from Michigan State University where my research focused on optimizing 3D printing process. With expertise in numerical methods and data analytics, I have experience in utilizing diverse techniques including time series analysis, finite element methods, regression modeling, and more. Being the first employee at my previous company, I've gained invaluable experience in learning different aspects of a business, allowing me to approach problems in holistic manner.

Outside work, I'm a chess enthusiast, avid reader, traveller, and enjoy spending time in nature. The experience of moving to countries where I knew no one and starting over has deeply instilled in me the importance of empathy, resilience, and developing meaningful relationships.
