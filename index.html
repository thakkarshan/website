About me

I earned my MS in mechanical engineering in May 2020 from Michigan State University where my research focused on optimizing 3D printing process. I've been working at the intersection of additive manufacturing and data science for real-time detection of anomalies to reduce scrap rate.

Trained in numerical methods and data analytics, I have experince working with large and varied datasets including time series analysis, optical and thermal image processing, anomaly detection to help businesses optimize the processes and drive data-driven decisions.

Phase identification
